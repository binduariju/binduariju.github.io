This paper proves new upper and lower bounds on $\epsilon$-approximate sign-rank, relaxing the concept introduced by Chornomaz, Moran, and Waknine (STOC 2025). The work establishes that $m \times n$ sign matrices with a specific approximate sign-rank contain large monochromatic rectangles. Furthermore, a new $\Omega(\sqrt{d/\log d})$ lower bound on the $\epsilon$-approximate sign-rank for large-margin $d$-dimensional half-spaces is demonstrated.
