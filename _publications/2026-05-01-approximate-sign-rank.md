We prove new upper and lower bounds on $\epsilon$-approximate sign-rank, a relaxation of sign-rank introduced by Chornomaz, Moran, and Waknine. We show that every $m \times n$ sign matrix with approximate sign-rank $d$ contains a monochromatic rectangle of size $d^{-O(d)}m \times d^{-O(d^2)}n$.
