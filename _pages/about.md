Hello and welcome! I am a Computer Science PhD student at **McGill University**, where I am incredibly fortunate to be co-supervised by [Hamed Hatami](https://mcgill.ca) and [Robert Robere](https://mcgill.ca). I am also a part of the **Complexity Group** here at McGill.

Mathematics has always been my first love. I have always been enthusiastic about exploring all its different sub-fields, and over the years, I have happily realized that I enjoy pretty much anything and everything that is combinatorial. 

Before moving to Montreal for my PhD, I completed my undergraduate degree at IIT Delhi. During my time there, I was blessed with wonderful mentors like Professor [Thomas Brüstle](https://usherbrooke.ca) and Professor [Naveen Garg](https://iitd.ac.in), who also kindly served as my undergraduate thesis advisor.

Away from the math, a big part of my life is just winding down with a good movie or series. When I am not caught up in a screen, I really love giving myself quiet moments to just introspect and philosophize about life. I also find a lot of peace simply wandering through local parks or heading out to catch a beautiful sunrise or sunset.

If you ever want to chat about complexity theory or anything combinatorial or swap notes on the absolute best spots in Montreal to watch the sun go down, please feel free to reach out!
